Jambalaya Recipe

Home

Ingredients

  • Butter: 40g
  • Sausage: 300g
  • Pork belly: 300g
  • Chicken thigh: 300g
  • Cajun seasoning: 3 table spoon
  • Onion: 150g
  • Pepper: 80g
  • Rice: 300g
  • Tomato: 150g
  • Tomato spaghetti sauce: 100g
  • Water: 360ml
  • Chicken broth: 5 table spoon
  • Sweet corn: 50g
  • Shirimps: 100g
  • Parcely: 7g

Steps

  1. Put butter, sausage, pork belly, chicken thigh and cajun seasoning into a pot and stir-fry until enough oil comes out from the meat and sausage.
  2. Add onion, pepper and rice, stir-fry until the onion becomes transparent.
  3. Add tomato, spaghetti sauce, water, chicken broth, sweet cornl shirimps and parcely, cover with a lid, and simmer on low heat for 30 minutes.
