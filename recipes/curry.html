Ingredients

  • Curry pack 12 servings
  • Chicken thigh: 600g
  • Onion: 3
  • Potato: 4
  • Carrot: 1
  • Garlic: 10
  • Butter: 40g
  • Water: 360ml
  • Coconut milk: 360ml
  • Tomato spaghetti sauce: 100g

Steps

  1. Prepare the onions, potatoes, carrot and garlics.
  2. Season the chicken with salt and pepper.
  3. Sear the chicken in a pan. Melt about 20g of butter.
  4. Saut√© the Vegetables.
  5. Blend the Vegetables.
  6. Combine and Simmer.
  7. Add Curry Roux.
  8. Incorporate Coconut Milk and Tomato Sauce.
  9. Prepare Garlic Chips.
  10. Serve.
